# []{#basicnet-mailnews-other}Other Mail and News Programs {#other-mail-and-news-programs .sect1}

::: {.sect1 lang="en"}
[Balsa-2.6.5](../xsoft/balsa.md "Balsa-2.6.5"){.xref} is a GTK2 based mail client.

[seamonkey-2.53.23](../xsoft/seamonkey.md "Seamonkey-2.53.23"){.xref} includes both a mail client and newsreader in its installation.

[Thunderbird-140.11.0esr](../xsoft/thunderbird.md "Thunderbird-140.11.0esr"){.xref} is a mail/news client based on the [Mozilla]{.application} code base.

[Evolution-3.60.2](../gnome/evolution.md "Evolution-3.60.2"){.xref} is a mail client and organizer from the [GNOME]{.application} desktop.
